HOME THEATER CONSIDERATIONS

Potomac Theater Installers will take an active role in walking you through the many aspects to consider when planning your ideal home theater experience.

A few key considerations include:

Layout

The layout, ceiling structure, acoustics, and lighting of the home theater room will help define your ultimate home viewing experience.

Display Options

Whether you prefer streaming or high-quality format viewing, your home theater display options, as well as speakers and the ideal receiver, can be customized for your setup.

Setup & Organization

Finally, we'll help you plan the best way to house all your new equipment, as well as how to furnish your space to create the home theater of your dreams.

The customization and creativity of your home theater starts with you. Potomac Theater Installers offers luxury features such as custom star field ceilings, lighted pathways, stages, and lighted step edges, candy and popcorn bars, and specialty themed rooms and décor.
